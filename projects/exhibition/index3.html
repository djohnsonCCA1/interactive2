About the exhibition— Edited

Exhibition 01:

Our first exhibition is focusing on the Parkside and Sunset district, and how the voice of the neighborhood can mirror the graffiti that’s present in those areas.

These districts sit on the edge of the most western part of San Francisco, because of their location and residents (families and older residents) they are know for being some of the quieter and safer neighborhoods in San Francisco. While our team was out surveying these districts they started to realize that the graffiti that was present in these districts really evoked this quiet voice that these districts are know for. The pieces they found were mostly small in scale and only one color, they almost blend into their environment.
